Personal Growth and Development

One of the most significant changes i've experienced during my time at Royal University of Phnom Penh is personal growth. Doing anything by myself and managing my own responsibilities has taught me independence and self-discipline, essential traits i now apply to my academic and personal life.

Being part of such a diverse community at RUPP has been eye-opening. Interacting with students from various cultural backgrounds has broadened my understanding of global perspectives and fostered a sense of inclusivity in my social and academic life.
